P2P & Data

Listam is built on a peer-to-peer (P2P) architecture. We believe that your personal data should remain yours.

All grocery lists and items are stored locally on your device. When you share a list, data is synced directly between peers using the Hypercore protocol. We do not operate central servers to store your list data, meaning Saynode Operations AG has absolutely no access to your list contents, habits, or personal information.

bar_chart

Analytics

We value your privacy and do not track your behavior within the app.

The only metrics we collect are aggregated, anonymous download statistics and standard crash reports provided directly by the App Store and Google Play Store. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers.

No Responsibility for Data Loss

Because Listam operates without a central server, you are the sole custodian of your data. If you lose your device or delete the application without a backup, your lists cannot be recovered by us. We highly recommend regular device backups.
